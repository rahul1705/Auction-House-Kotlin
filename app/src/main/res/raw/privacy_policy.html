Auction Hub Privacy Policy

Auction Hub is an Android application that allows users to participate in online auctions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

When you use Auction Hub, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Your name, mobile number and email address.
  • Information about the items you post for auction or bid on.
  • Your payment information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide and improve the Auction Hub app, including:

  • Facilitating auctions and bids.
  • Communicating with you about auctions and bids.
  • Analyzing app usage and improving our services.
  • Sending promotional emails or push notifications with your consent.
  • Complying with legal obligations.

How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following ways:

  • With service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, data analysis, and customer service.
  • In response to legal process, such as a court order or subpoena.
  • If we believe disclosure is necessary or appropriate to protect the rights, property, or safety of Auction Hub, our customers, or others.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@auctionhub.com.
